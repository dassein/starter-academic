Purpose: 期望最⼤化算法

maxln⁡p(X|θ)={∑Zq(Z)}ln⁡p(X|θ)={∑Zq(Z)}ln⁡p(X,Z|θ)p(Z|X,θ)=L(q,θ)+KL(q∥p)

where X is constant, parameter q(Z),θ, random variable Z;

X,Z are discrete matrix

L(q,θ)=∑Zq(Z)ln⁡{p(X,Z|θ)q(Z)}KL(q∥p)=−∑Zq(Z)ln⁡{p(Z|X,θ)q(Z)}=−∑Zq(Z)ln⁡{q(Z)}+∑Zq(Z)ln⁡{p(Z|X,θ)}≥0

Where

  • q(Z) is ∀ arbitrary distribution for Z
  • L(q,θ) 概率分布q(Z)的⼀个泛函, θ 函数
  • KL(q∥p) 概率分布q(Z)的⼀个泛函, θ 函数，KL散度 of p(Z|X,θ), q(Z), ≥0
